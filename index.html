Who We Are

A home for creativity in Uttara

Rangon (রঙন) — meaning "to colour" — was founded on May 1, 2013. We are Uttara's premier creative institute offering a wide range of programs in visual arts, music, dance, and crafts in a warm, nurturing environment where every child blossoms.

🎓

Expert instructors

Practising artists and musicians with formal training and a genuine passion for teaching children.

🏫

Vibrant classrooms

Professional materials, instruments, and bright spaces designed specifically to inspire young minds.

👨‍👩‍👧

Personalised learning

We work closely with parents to understand each child's unique goals, pace, and personality.

Our Philosophy

What we believe

🌟
Every child is unique

We believe every child has the potential to be creative. Our goal is to provide a platform to explore and express creativity through art, music, and crafts — at their own pace, in their own way.

01
🎯
Learning should be joyful

We create a positive, stimulating environment where learning is interactive and fun. Our bright classrooms and wide range of materials make every class an adventure worth remembering.

02
🚀
Creativity drives everything

Creativity isn't just art — it's thinking differently and being innovative. We develop these lifelong skills and instil a deep love for learning in every student at Rangon.
